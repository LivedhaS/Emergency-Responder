🔥 Overview

Burns are injuries caused by heat, chemicals, electricity, sunlight, or radiation. They range from mild to life-threatening and require appropriate care.

Frequently Asked Questions

🩹 What should I do immediately after getting a burn? ▾
Cool the burn under running water for at least 10 minutes and cover it with a clean cloth.
📌 When should I seek medical help for a burn? ▾
If the burn is deep, larger than a palm, or located on the face, hands, feet, or genitals.
⚠ Is it safe to pop burn blisters? ▾
No. Let blisters heal naturally to avoid infection.
